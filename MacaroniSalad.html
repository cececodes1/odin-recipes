Tuna Macaroni Salad

This tuna macaroni salad is easy to make. It will remind you of Grandma's!

Ingredients

  • 1 paackage elbow macaroni
  • 1 can baby peas, drained
  • 2 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 2 tablespoons chopped sweet onion
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
  • 3 large hard-cooked eggs, quarted
  • 1 pinch paprika for garnish
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and rinse under cold running water
  2. Stir together macaroni, peas, celery, tuna, and onion in a large bowl
  3. Mix in mayonnaise, relish, salt and pepper
  4. Garnish with egg wedges and sprinkle of paprika. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour before serving
