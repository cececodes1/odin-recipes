Pasta Salad

This is a classic asain pasta salad recipe with a variety of veggies and toasted sesame seeds

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • olive olive
  • 6 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • rice vinegar
  • toasted sesame seeds
  • sweet chilli sauce
  • chopped onions
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in boiling water for 10-12 minutes
  2. Whisk soy sauce, sugar, vinegar, sesame seeds, chilli sause, and sesame oil together in a bowl until sugar dissolves. Toss soy sauce mixture with pasta; top with green onions, red bell pepper, and snap peas.
